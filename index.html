Influencer Collaboration

Recognizable faces add a touch of familiarity and increase viewer interest, making these videos a hit among the audience.

Possible benefits of such video:

Reach: The reach of a collaboration can range from a few thousands to few millions of potential impressions, depending on the influencer's engagement rate and visibility of the content. It's normal to achieve a reach of 500,000 to 1 million people with a macro influencer promotion.

Impressions: The number of impressions can range from tens of thousands to several hundred thousand or even millions.

Engagement: On average, an engagement rate of 2-5% is considered reasonable. So, for an influencer with 1 million followers, we can expect to receive anywhere from 20,000 to 50,000 engagements (likes, comments, shares, etc.).

Website Traffic and Conversions: we could potentially experience thousands of visitors to our website which would lead to new Hostinger customers.
